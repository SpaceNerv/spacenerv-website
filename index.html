What it is

Every tap is a photograph
taken in darkness.

Passive NFC chips and QR codes embedded in architectural surfaces act as sensing nodes. Human operators carrying smartphones serve as the data transmission channel. The tap event is simultaneously the physical operational action and its authenticated digital record.

No dedicated hardware beyond the chip. No app install required. Works on any NFC-enabled smartphone or camera phone. The intelligence emerges from the aggregate.
